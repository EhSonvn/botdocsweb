Our bot

Our bot is made for you and only you. Using weather api and various libraries, you can now find out more about weather information in your place, or even all over the world, there are no limits for you to find out more about weather information of all the cities, from ShangHai to San Francisco, there are no cities that Alexis can't search, you now know everything about the weather of that city, from temperature to windspeed, wind direction,etc...

Not only that...

You can also view a datetime accurately using epoch time (unix time), from there you can view your time not only in hours, in minutes, but in seconds, tickling everytime.

Well, that's it...

We know the bot is still having bugs or only a few features for you to choose from, but by the time this website is being made, the project is only 4 days old. With you support, we will continue to improve your user experience and adding more features, so what you gonna say?
